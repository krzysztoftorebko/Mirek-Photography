Brief Story

I started shooting photos many years ago using an old Zenith, a great piece of equipment, even though it wasn't digital, quality was comparable to what we can achive today. Of course there was no photoshop back in the days - and you couldn't see a shot before you entered the dark room - but it made it all more mysterious for me.
